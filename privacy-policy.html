Privacy Policy

The Emoji Copy Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The only data saved is a simple on/off toggle stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local. This setting is used to enable or disable the extension and is not synced or sent anywhere.

The extension reads Emojipedia links on Google Search results to fetch emojis and display a copy button. No search queries, browsing history, or user input is stored or monitored.

I do not collect or access any personal information, location data, web history, or user activity.
